Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NoteSnap Pro

When will NoteSnap Pro ship? +

We plan to ship all rewards within 3 months after the crowdfunding campaign ends. You'll receive tracking information via email.

Is the metal surface really erasable? +

Yes! The premium aluminum surface allows infinite write-wipe-rewrite cycles. Use the included eraser or any soft cloth to wipe clean instantly.

Does it work with all phones? +

NoteSnap Pro uses ultra-strong MagSafe technology and works with all MagSafe-compatible phones (iPhone 12 and later). For other phones, we include an adhesive adapter.

What colors are available? +

We offer 3 colorway series: Classic Black, Midnight Blue, and Rose Gold. Team Pack backers can choose custom colors.
